📖 How to Play Letter-Leap

🎯 Objective

The goal is simple: you’re in a race against time to type as many real English words as you can using the letters on screen. Think of it like word sprinting—every correct word gives you points, and longer words score more. But here’s the twist: you only get credit for words with 3 letters or more. So no, “us” won’t cut it. 😏

Just type a word, hit Enter, and boom—if it’s valid, it gets added to your score. If not? You get a pop-up calling you out (nicely). The more words you find, and the smarter your word choices, the higher your score. Easy to play, tough to master.

🕹️ Game Modes

  • Single Player Mode: 60 seconds to type all the words you can find. Only 4+ letter words allowed.
  • Multiplayer Mode: Join a room and face a friend. Room owner can activate AI for a 3rd player.

⌨️ Controls

Type a word using the letters. Hit Enter to submit. Get points for correct words. Invalid ones trigger a pop-up!

🏆 Achievements

  • 🧠 Word Wizard: 50+ unique words
  • 💨 Speed Demon: 5 words in 10s
  • 🐢 Late Bloomer: Strong finish
  • 😂 Funny feedback on playstyle

💡 Pro Tips

  • Don’t rush; type real words
  • Time & accuracy matter
  • Practice makes perfect, fr!
